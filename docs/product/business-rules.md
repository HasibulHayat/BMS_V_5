# Business Rules

## Ownership

* Multiple owners allowed
* Total ownership must not exceed 100%
* Ownership is time-based

---

## Lease

* Only one active lease per apartment
* Apartment must have at least one owner
* Resident must be defined

---

## Users

* Email, username, and phone must be unique
* Users must be active to access system

---

## Access Control

* Actions are restricted based on roles
* Unauthorized access is blocked
