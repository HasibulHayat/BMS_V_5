# Product Overview

## What is this system?

The Building Management System (BMS) is designed to simplify property management by digitizing and automating operations related to buildings, apartments, ownership, and tenants.

---

## Who is it for?

* Property management companies
* Real estate developers
* Building administrators
* Apartment owners

---

## Key Goals

* Reduce manual work and errors
* Centralize all building data
* Provide clear ownership tracking
* Simplify tenant and lease management
* Enable secure access control

---

## Core Modules

* Building Management
* Apartment Management
* Ownership Management
* Lease Management
* User & Role Management
